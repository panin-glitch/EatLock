## Description

TadLock helps you build more intentional meals with simple before-and-after meal sessions.

With TadLock you can:

- scan meals before eating and review estimated calories, protein, carbs, and fat;
- edit nutrition details when you want to correct the estimate;
- track meal-session time and post-meal distraction ratings;
- view streak progress and meal history;
- unlock TadLock Pro for premium meal-scanning workflows.

Important platform note:

- The current iOS build does not enforce device-level app blocking. Any focus or app-selection flows on iOS are informational only and should not be described as device-level blocking in App Store screenshots, subtitle, or description.
- The current iOS build should not be marketed as providing live reminder notifications unless and until notifications are implemented in the shipped iOS binary.
