# TadLock Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 23, 2026

## Overview

TadLock helps users scan meals, review nutrition details, track meal-session progress, and manage TadLock Pro subscriptions. This Privacy Policy explains what information TadLock collects, how it is used, and which service providers process it.

## Information We Collect

- Account information: email address, authentication identifiers, and profile data used to create and manage your TadLock account.
- Meal session data: meal type, timestamps, distraction ratings, streak progress, and nutrition details you edit or save in the app.
- Meal photos and barcode data: before/after meal images, barcode scans, and food-label information submitted for nutrition analysis.
- Subscription status: purchase and entitlement status used to determine access to TadLock Pro features.
- App settings: nutrition goals, macro preferences, language choice, and other local app preferences.

## How We Use Information

We use this information to:

- authenticate users and keep accounts signed in securely;
- provide meal scanning, nutrition estimation, and meal-session summaries;
- store meal history, streak data, and user preferences;
- verify TadLock Pro subscription access and restore purchases;
- improve app reliability, security, and abuse prevention.

## Service Providers

TadLock uses third-party services to operate the app:

- Supabase for authentication, account records, and application data storage.
- Cloudflare Workers for TadLock backend API processing.
- OpenAI for meal-photo and nutrition analysis requests sent through the TadLock backend.
- RevenueCat for subscription entitlement management and purchase restoration.
- Apple for App Store payments and subscription billing on iOS.

## Photos and Nutrition Analysis

If you choose to scan a meal, TadLock sends the relevant meal image or barcode payload to its backend services so nutrition analysis can be performed. These requests are used only to provide the feature and related app functionality.

## Subscription Billing

Apple processes iOS subscription payments. TadLock does not receive your full payment card details. RevenueCat and Apple provide subscription status information so the app can determine whether TadLock Pro access is active.

## Data Retention

Account and meal data are retained until you delete them or delete your account, unless retention is required for security, fraud prevention, legal compliance, or dispute handling.

## Account Deletion

You can request account deletion from within the app. Deleting your account removes your account record and associated cloud data that TadLock stores, subject to any legal retention obligations. If you have an active Apple subscription, billing continues through Apple until you cancel it in App Store subscriptions.

## Security

TadLock uses reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect user information, including authenticated API access and secure session storage where supported. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

## Children's Privacy

TadLock is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## International Processing

Your information may be processed in countries other than the one where you live, including countries where TadLock and its service providers operate infrastructure.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the effective date above.
